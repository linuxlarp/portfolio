Southwest Airlines PTFS 󰋑

SWAPTFS was a personal project, attempting to realistically recreate the operations of the IRL Southwest Airlines in Roblox's PTFS (Pilot Training Flight Simulator)

Key Contirbutions:
- Using Photoshop to design graphics, including advertisements and branding assets.
- Using Discord to manage and moderate the community.
- Using Python  , TypeScript , JavaScript , FastAPI  & NextJS  to build and maintain the group's website.
- Using aviation knowledge to develop flight procedures and staff documentation.

The group ran for approximately a year before I stepped down due to personal reasons. It recieved community recognition through nominations for "Best Technology" and "Best Airline", and grew to nearly 1,000 members before leadership was transferred.

The code for the backend of the website is now available for free under the %LINK:MIT:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MIT_License% license.
